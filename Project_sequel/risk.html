Acidity tests in Gurgaon

Acidity is quite a common condition; in fact it is so common that most of us have experienced it in our lives. Though acidity is quite common, not many people know the actual reason why it happens. A ring of muscle called the lower esophageal sphincter acts as a valve at the entry of your stomach. Usually it closes as soon as a person consumes food, however, if there is a problem in it closing or if it opens up too often then the acid produced in the stomach moves up to the oesophagus causing a burning sensation known as acidity. If acidity occurs more than twice a week then it might indicate gastroesophageal reflux disease (GERD). If you suffer from acidity regularly then getting an Acidity Test is the best way to check if you suffer from GERD or if there is some other reason for it. While normally acidity is easily diagnosable and a person suffering from it can identify it easily, an acidity Test in Gurgaon usually includes other tests to check for impact as well as other underlying causes. The phlebotomist takes your blood sample, which takes only a few minutes. The sample is then sent to the lab to perform the tests included in an Acidity Test package.

Some symptoms that people with GERD may experience are:

  • Heartburn
  • Regurgitation
  • Bloating
  • Burping
  • Nausea
  • Wheezing, dry cough
  • Unexplained weight loss
  • Dysphagia

Getting an Acidity Test in Gurgaon is the best way to keep track of your condition and take the necessary steps before it worsens.

Why Acidity Testing is important?

While acidity in itself is common and resolves fairly easily since there are many instant heartburn relief medications available, regular recurrence can indicate a more severe health issue as well as cause disruptions in daily life. Getting an acidity test in Gurgaon done in the early stages and then regularly following up with the doctor can help you take the necessary steps in improving or even resolving your condition.

Some common risk factors of acidity or GERD are:

  • Eating big meals or lying down immediately after a meal
  • Being obese or overweight
  • Snacking just before bedtime
  • Eating specific food like citrus, tomato, chocolate, mint, garlic, onions, or spicy or fatty foods
  • Drinking alcohol, caffeinated beverages
  • Smoking
  • Being pregnant

Getting acidity Tests in Gurgaon is also important because severe heartburn can mimic the symptoms of heart attack. Regular health checkups keep you on top of your condition and help you take immediate action in case of emergency.

How Acidity Test is performed in Gurgaon?

A doctor usually diagnoses acidity by asking you about the symptoms, their frequency and intensity and may advise further tests. Once acidity is confirmed, a doctor may recommend further Acidity Tests to look for underlying causes or its impact. These may include blood tests like liver function test, lipid profile, homocysteine, etc.

Early detection through an acidity test in Gurgaon can help you avoid many major conditions by taking necessary medications and implementing lifestyle changes.
